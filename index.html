Welcome to BookBank

The one stop shop for reused books

What do we do?

BookBank is a way of reusing books. See, once students use books, they have almost no use of them. So what I plan to do is make use of these old books. My goal is a system which reuses books and provides them for the next year's students as a very low price. This helps save hundreds of trees each year, and allows books to be bought as extremely low prices.
